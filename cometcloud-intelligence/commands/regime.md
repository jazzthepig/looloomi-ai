# /regime — Current Macro Regime

**Usage:** `/regime`

**What it returns:**
- Current macro regime (RISK_ON / RISK_OFF / GOLDILOCKS / TIGHTENING / EASING / STAGFLATION)
- Fear & Greed Index value and label
- BTC price and dominance
- Total market cap
- 2-sentence interpretation of what this regime means for CIS positioning

**API:** `GET https://looloomi.ai/api/v1/market/macro-pulse`

**Compliance:** Regime information is presented as market intelligence, not investment advice.
